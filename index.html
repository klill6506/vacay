The Lill's 2025 Virgin Islands Vacation

June 20 – Arrival

  • Arrive at STT at 3:00 PM
  • Transfer via St. John Villas private ferry
  • Check-in: Westin St. John Resort Villas
  • Dinner: The Longboard

June 21 – Explore St. John

  • Hike in Virgin Islands National Park
  • Snorkeling at Trunk Bay
  • Dinner: Extra Virgin Bistro

June 22 – Adventure & Relax

  • Kayaking at Hurricane Hole or Paddleboarding at Maho Bay
  • Beach time at Honeymoon Beach
  • Dinner: The Terrace Restaurant

June 23 – Travel to St. Thomas

  • Ferry to St. Thomas
  • Check-in: Buoy Haus Beach Resort
  • Dinner: Mim’s Seaside Bistro

June 24 – Zipline & Beach

  • Ziplining: Tree Limin’ Extreme
  • Beach time at Magens Bay
  • Dinner: Old Stone Farmhouse

June 25 – Final Day Fun

  • Shopping in Charlotte Amalie
  • Beach at Lindquist Beach or Secret Harbour
  • Dinner: Prime at Paradise Point

June 26 – Departure

  • Check out from Buoy Haus
  • Be at STT Airport by 11:30 AM for 1:30 PM flight
