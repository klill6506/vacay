üöá Boston Transportation (The "T"):

Your Hotel Location: Courtyard Boston Downtown/North Station is directly connected to North Station - one of Boston's major transit hubs!

The MBTA System:

  • At North Station (your hotel): Orange Line and Green Line subway, plus Commuter Rail
  • Fare: $2.40 per subway ride with CharlieCard, $2.90 with CharlieTicket or cash
  • CharlieCard: Reusable card available free at station kiosks - best value!
  • Day Pass: $11 for unlimited subway and local bus rides
  • 7-Day Pass: $22.50 (probably not worth it for your stay)

Key Lines from North Station:

  • Orange Line: Goes to Downtown Crossing, Chinatown, Back Bay, Museum of Fine Arts area
  • Green Line: Goes to Boston Common, Public Garden, Copley Square, Newbury Street
  • Commuter Rail: To Salem (for your Friday trip!), other suburbs

Getting to Key Destinations:

  • Freedom Trail/Faneuil Hall: Walk (15 min) or Orange Line to State/Haymarket
  • North End (Italian restaurants): Walk from hotel (10-15 minutes)
  • Newbury Street: Green Line to Arlington or Copley
  • Museum of Fine Arts: Green Line E to Museum of Fine Arts stop
  • Boston Common/Public Garden: Green Line to Boylston or Park Street
  • Seaport District: Walk to South Station (Orange Line), then Silver Line SL1 or SL2
  • Logan Airport: Blue Line from State St, or Silver Line from South Station (free!)
