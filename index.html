The Lill's 2025 Virgin Islands Vacation

Friday, June 20 — Arrival & St. John

  • 3:00 pmArrive St. Thomas (STT)
  • Pick up 4WD SUV from Enterprise at Cyril E. King Airport
  • Drive to Red Hook, take public ferry to Cruz Bay, St. John
  • EveningCheck in: Westin St. John Resort Villas
  • Dinner: The Longboard or Morgan's Mango

Saturday, June 21 — St. John Adventure

  • Morning: Relax at Trunk Bay or Cinnamon Bay
  • Lunch: Lime Out VI (taco boat) (allow time for boat trip out and back) or Morgan's Mango
  • Afternoon: Kayak or paddleboard with St. John Kayak (book ahead) or hike a trail in Virgin Islands National Park
  • Dinner: Sunset Grille (nice view, not overly pricey) or Extra Virgin Bistro

Sunday, June 22 — More St. John

  • Morning: Pool or beach day at the Westin, or visit Maho Bay (gentle snorkeling, turtles)
  • Lunch: Sam & Jack's Deli or Deli Grotto
  • Afternoon: Explore Cruz Bay shops or relax at Hawksnest Bay
  • Dinner: High Tide Bar & Grill or The Tap Room

Monday, June 23 — St. John to St. Thomas

  • Morning: Ferry to St. Thomas (drive 4WD SUV to Red Hook, take ferry, then drive to hotel)
  • Check in: Buoy Haus Beach Resort (after 4:00 pm)
  • Lunch: Hull Bay Hideaway or Surf N' Turf Bistro
  • Afternoon: Ziplining at Tree Limin' Extreme or relax at Magens Bay
  • Dinner: The Drake's Passage Bar & Grill or Havana Blue

Tuesday, June 24 — St. Thomas

  • Morning: Beach time at Magens Bay or Secret Harbour
  • Lunch: Bistro on the Bay or Iggies Beach Bar & Grill
  • Afternoon: Explore Charlotte Amalie shops or take a sightseeing boat tour
  • Dinner: Havana Blue or Oceana

Wednesday, June 25 — Last Day

  • Morning: Relax at Buoy Haus pool/beach or rent kayaks at Bon Vivant
  • Lunch: Frenchtown Brewery or Grasshopper Bar
  • Afternoon: Optional water sports or visit Coral World Ocean Park
  • Dinner: The Drake's Passage Bar & Grill or local food at Iggies Beach Bar & Grill

Thursday, June 26 — Departure

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel or nearby
  • 1:30 pmDepart St. Thomas (STT) on Delta
